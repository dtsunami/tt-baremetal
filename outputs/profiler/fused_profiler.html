One fused multi-tile training step — x280 projection + backward + Adam over GDDR-resident Gaussians, per-tile Tensix render/backward across the worker grid, per-Gaussian grad scatter-add. Every stage is timed. The cycle counters say the silicon compute is nearly free; the wall-clock is spent on the host↔device exalens relay. That gap is the whole tuning opportunity.
