hart 3 is the weak die

It sets Vmin at every frequency; hart 0 is next (both fail at 2500 MHz). All four share one PLL & one rail, so hart 3 is the limiter.

Binary search, from above

Voltage leads frequency, then step down to the first soft error. Approaching the edge from above avoids hard-wedging a weak hart on the shared rail.

2.4 GHz is the firmware ceiling

2400 MHz needs 882 mV (fits under vdd_max=900). 2500 needs ~919 → blocked. Reaching it means a reversible SPIROM ChipLimits edit toward 1.0 V.

Thermals not the limiter

Peak 74 °C at the edge — well under the 90 °C throttle. Voltage headroom, not heat, is the wall.
